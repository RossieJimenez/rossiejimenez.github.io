About Us

Hello! Thank you for visiting our home price prediction website. We are Rossie, Jessica, Rodney, and Francesca, the team behind this platform. Our goal is to help you estimate the cost of your dream home, providing insights into how much you might need to save and giving you peace of mind about your future homeâ€™s cost.

Our journey began with gathering and cleaning a dataset that included features such as price, bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, lot size, floors, waterfront presence, view, year built, year renovated, zipcode, latitude, longitude, and renovation category. After removing duplicates and handling missing values, we conducted exploratory analyses to identify useful information for prediction.

We started with univariate analysis, creating histograms and bar plots to understand the frequency of each column. Next, we performed bivariate scatter plots to explore relationships between two variables, such as price and square footage. Finally, we conducted multivariate analysis to show relationships among three variables, like price, square footage, and view, using color for the third variable.

Initially, we experimented with a KMeans model, but the results were unsatisfactory. We then shifted to a Random Forest Regressor, selecting features such as bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, floors, waterfront presence, view, year built, and zipcode. This model provided more accurate and reliable predictions.

Our website aims to empower you with knowledge about the real estate market, helping you make informed financial decisions. We hope you find our tool helpful. Thank you for trusting us to assist you in this important journey.

Feel free to explore our site, use our prediction tool, and contact us with any questions or feedback.
